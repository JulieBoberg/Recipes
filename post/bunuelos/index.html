Bunuelos

Posted on 2023, Jan 04 One min read

Ingredients

Dough

  • 3 cups of flour
  • 1 tsp of salt
  • Water
  • Tomatillo leaves

Syrup

  • Piloncillo
  • Cinnamon sticks
  • Orange peels

Recipe

  1. For dough, boil water with tomatillo husks. Strain the husks out.
  2. Add water slowly to dry ingredients as needed.
  3. Roll dough into approximately a dozen balls.
  4. Let rest for 30 minutes.
  5. In a castiron skillet add water, cinnamon sticks, orange peels, and piloncillo.
  6. Simmer until piloncillo disolves.
  7. In a seperate skillet heat oil for frying.
  8. Roll out dough and stretch by hand until thin.
  9. Gently place tortilla in hot oil and fry both sides.
  10. Place on paper towel when done to soak up excess oil.
  11. Once drained, place fried dough in piloncillo syrup on both sides.
  12. Place on a plate in a stack.

Notes

  • Questions:

    • How long do you boil the tomatillo husks before straining?
    • Approx how much water goes in syrup?
