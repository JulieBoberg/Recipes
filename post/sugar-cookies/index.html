Sugar Cookies

Posted on 2023, Jan 04 One min read

Ingredients

  • 1 cup butter
  • 1 cup of sugar
  • 1 large egg
  • 1 tsp vanilla
  • 2 tsp baking powder
  • 3 cups of flour
  • 3 tbsp milk

Recipe

  1. Preheat oven to 400 degrees
  2. Beat butter and sugar in bowl until creamy.
  3. Add egg and vanilla.
  4. On low speed mix baking powder and flour into butter mixture until combined.
  5. Add milk and beat until dough collects around beaters (approx 2 minutes).
  6. Divide dough into thirds.
  7. Roll out each third of dough to 1/3 inch thickness on lightly floured surface.
  8. Cut into shapes and bake for 10 minutes.

Notes

  • Be sure to bake Michael’s dinosaur cookie
